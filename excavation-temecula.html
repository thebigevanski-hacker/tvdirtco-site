Our Excavation Services

Pool Excavation

Expert pool digging for inground pools of all shapes and sizes. We work with leading pool contractors throughout Temecula Valley to ensure precise excavation that meets engineering specifications.

  • ✓ Standard rectangle and freeform pools
  • ✓ Gunite, fiberglass, and vinyl liner pools
  • ✓ Hillside and sloped yard excavation
  • ✓ Soil removal and disposal included
  • ✓ Utility line marking and protection

Foundation & Footing Excavation

Precision foundation excavation for new construction, room additions, and structural improvements. We follow engineering plans exactly and work closely with builders and inspectors.

  • ✓ House foundations and basements
  • ✓ Room additions and ADUs
  • ✓ Retaining wall footings
  • ✓ Deck and patio foundations
  • ✓ Precise depth and grade control

Trenching Services

Professional trenching for utilities, drainage, and irrigation systems. We dig clean, precise trenches and backfill properly to prevent future settling.

  • ✓ Water and sewer lines
  • ✓ Electrical and gas utility trenches
  • ✓ French drains and drainage systems
  • ✓ Irrigation main lines
  • ✓ Fiber optic and cable trenches
