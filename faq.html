How much does excavation cost in Temecula?

Pool excavation: Typically $2,500-$5,000 depending on size (small plunge pool vs large lap pool) and site access.

Foundation excavation: Averages $1,500-$3,500 for standard residential foundations. Larger or deeper foundations cost more.

Trenching: $3-$7 per linear foot depending on depth and soil conditions.

We provide free quotes with exact pricing based on your project specifications, site conditions, and Temecula area accessibility.

Do I need a permit for land clearing in Murrieta?

Brush clearing / weed abatement: Generally no permit required for fire prevention clearing on your own property.

Grading: Permits typically required if moving more than 50 cubic yards of soil or changing property drainage.

Tree removal: Check with Murrieta city ordinances—some protected species require permits.

We help determine permit requirements and can recommend local permit services. Always better to check first!

What's the difference between a mini excavator and skid steer?

Mini Excavator: Best for digging — pools, trenches, foundations, hillside work. Digs vertically with precision. Has a boom and bucket that reaches down and out.

Skid Steer: Best for moving materials — grading, leveling, hauling dirt, spreading gravel. Works horizontally at ground level. Great for surface work and pushing.

Think of it this way: Need to dig a hole? Mini excavator. Need to move dirt piles or level ground? Skid steer.

Not sure? Call us at 951-555-DIRT and we'll help you choose!

How long does pool excavation take?

Most residential pools: 1-2 days
Small plunge pools: 1 day
Large lap pools: 2-3 days
Hillside / difficult access: Add 1 day

Factors include pool size, shape complexity, soil conditions (rock vs soft soil), site access, and weather. We work efficiently to keep your project on schedule and coordinate with your pool contractor.
