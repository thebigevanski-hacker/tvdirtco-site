Your pool deck is dated, cracked, or needs replacement for a complete backyard redesign. Maybe you're upgrading from old concrete to modern pavers or travertine. Pool deck removal is high-stakes work. We work within inches of pool edges, around plumbing lines, near gas lines for heaters, and alongside electrical conduits for pumps and lighting. Pool Deck Types We Remove

🏊 Concrete Pool Decks

Standard poured concrete, stamped concrete, or acid-stained finishes. Most common in Temecula. We break, haul, and dispose of all concrete debris.

🧱 Paver Pool Decks

Brick pavers, concrete pavers, or interlocking systems. We remove pavers, base material, and underlying concrete if present. Pavers can be salvaged if desired.

🪨 Natural Stone Decks

Flagstone, travertine, slate, or bluestone. We carefully remove stone (can salvage for reuse) and demolish the concrete base underneath.

🎨 Tile Pool Decks

Ceramic, porcelain, or specialty pool tile. We remove tile and mortar bed, then demo the concrete slab beneath. Dusty work requiring containment.
