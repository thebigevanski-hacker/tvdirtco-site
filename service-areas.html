Why California Dirt Co. Is Different

We're not another franchise dispatching random crews. We're building something real.

🎯

Local Crews, Not National Dispatch

Every market gets dedicated local crews who live and work in that community. When you call Orange County Dirt Co., you get Orange County crews. When you call San Diego Dirt Co., you get San Diego crews. No outsourcing, no subcontracting to the lowest bidder, no dispatching random guys from three cities away.

⚙️

Professional Equipment, Not Rentals

We own our equipment—modern excavators, skid steers with hydraulic breakers, dump trailers, and concrete saws. Not rental junk that breaks mid-job. Our crews show up with professional tools ready to work, not cobbled-together equipment from Home Depot.

💰

Transparent Pricing, Not Hidden Fees

We tell you the price upfront—written quotes with clear line items. No surprise charges for "access fees," no inflated dump fees, no BS upsells. You know what you're paying before we start. We include everything: breaking, hauling, disposal, cleanup. One price, done.

🤝

Owner-Operated, Not Corporate

When you call 951-517-DIRT, you talk to the owner or local crew lead. Not a call center in another state. Not a sales rep who's never touched a shovel. We answer our phones, we show up when we say we will, and we stand behind our work because our reputation is everything.
