Security Deposit Policy

Last Updated: February 6, 2026

What is a Security Deposit?

A security deposit is a hold (authorization) placed on your payment method before equipment is delivered or picked up. This is NOT a charge. The funds remain in your account but are temporarily reserved.

The deposit protects TVDirtCo against damage, loss, theft, late returns, or other violations of the rental agreement. When equipment is returned in good condition, on time, and clean, the hold is released within 3-5 business days.

Security Deposit Amounts

Standard Deposits (Rentals Under 7 Days)

Equipment Security Deposit
Mini Excavator (Cat 301.7 CR) $1,000
Skid Steer (2TS/215) $800
Dump Trailer (6x12) $500

Extended Rentals (7+ Days)

For rentals of 7 days or longer, the security deposit equals 100% of the total rental cost.

Example: 10-day rental totaling $2,500 = $2,500 security deposit hold

This protects against extended wear, increased damage exposure, and ensures commitment for longer rentals.

When is the Deposit Held?

The security deposit authorization is placed on your payment method:

  • Before delivery: When we confirm your booking and process payment
  • Before pickup: When you arrive to collect equipment from our yard

You will see the hold appear on your credit/debit card statement as a "pending" or "authorization" charge.

When is the Deposit Released?

The security deposit hold is released when:

  1. Equipment is returned by the agreed-upon date and time
  2. Equipment passes our return inspection (no damage beyond normal wear)
  3. Equipment is returned clean and with full fuel tank
  4. All attachments and accessories are returned
  5. No rental agreement violations occurred

Release timeframe: 3-5 business days after equipment return. The exact timing depends on your bank/card issuer.

What Can the Deposit Be Used For?

The security deposit may be charged (in full or in part) to cover:

1. Equipment Damage

Repair or replacement costs for damage beyond normal wear and tear, including broken parts, bent components, hydraulic leaks, etc.

2. Missing Parts or Accessories

Replacement cost for missing buckets, attachments, keys, manuals, or other items provided with equipment.

3. Excessive Cleaning

Cleaning fees if equipment is returned excessively dirty (caked mud, concrete, chemicals). Standard jobsite dirt is expected and acceptable.

4. Late Return Fees

Additional rental charges for late returns. Daily rate applies for each day equipment is not returned.

5. Fuel Charges

If equipment is not returned with a full tank: $5/gallon + $25 service charge.

6. Theft or Total Loss

Full replacement cost if equipment is stolen or deemed a total loss. Deposit is charged immediately; balance may be invoiced separately.

Certificate of Insurance (COI) - Waive the Deposit

✓ DEPOSIT WAIVED with Valid COI

If you provide a valid Certificate of Insurance that meets our requirements, the security deposit hold is completely waived.

COI Requirements:

Your Certificate of Insurance must include:

  • Commercial General Liability (CGL) coverage
  • Equipment coverage for rented machinery/equipment
  • Temecula Valley Dirt Co. listed as:
    • Additional Insured
    • Loss Payee for rented equipment
  • Coverage amounts: Minimum $1M general liability recommended
  • Current policy dates: Must cover your rental period

How to provide COI: Upload during booking process, or email to info@tvdirtco.com with your booking confirmation number. We verify within 24 hours.

Damage Waiver vs. Security Deposit

These are two separate policies that work together:

Security Deposit Damage Waiver
Purpose Guarantee against loss/damage Reduce your liability
Cost $0 (held, not charged) 12% of rental (optional)
Required? Yes (unless COI provided) No (optional)
Covers All costs up to deposit amount Covered damage only
When charged Only if damage/issues occur Paid upfront, non-refundable

Example scenario: You rent a Mini Excavator ($1,000 deposit) and purchase Damage Waiver ($120). If covered accidental damage occurs, you pay $0 and your deposit is not charged. Without the waiver, repair costs would be deducted from your deposit.

Dispute Resolution

If you disagree with any charges against your security deposit:

  1. We provide detailed documentation (photos, repair invoices, itemized charges)
  2. You have 5 business days to dispute in writing
  3. We review your dispute and supporting evidence
  4. We respond within 10 business days with resolution
  5. If unresolved, mediation or small claims court may be pursued

We document all equipment condition with photos before and after each rental to ensure fairness.

Important Notes

  • Security deposit is separate from rental payment - both are required
  • Deposit amounts are non-negotiable (except COI waiver)
  • Bank holds may take 3-5 business days to release (not under our control)
  • Deposit does NOT cover third-party liability (injury/property damage to others)
  • Multiple rentals = separate deposit for each piece of equipment
  • Deposit policy applies to all customers equally
