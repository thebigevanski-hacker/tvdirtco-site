Concrete Removal Services in Temecula CA

Temecula Valley Dirt Co provides professional concrete demolition throughout Temecula and the surrounding valley communities including Murrieta, Menifee, Winchester, and Wildomar. Whether you're removing an old driveway before installing pavers, demolishing a cracked patio, or clearing a concrete slab for construction, our team handles the entire process from demolition to haul-off and cleanup.

Our crew uses professional demolition equipment designed for residential concrete removal. Hydraulic breakers and compact excavation equipment allow us to break up thick concrete slabs quickly while minimizing disruption to surrounding landscaping or structures.

Every project includes full loading, hauling, and responsible disposal of broken concrete at approved recycling facilities.
