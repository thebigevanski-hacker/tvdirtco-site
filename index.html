About TV Dirt Co.

When life gets messy, we say… Rub Some Dirt On It! That’s how Temecula Valley Dirt Co. got started, tackling tough jobs, learning as we go, and never shying away from a little hard work. From small backyard remodels to full scale commercial projects across Temecula, Riverside, and San Diego counties, we bring precision, reliability, and a hands on approach to every job. We’re not just contractors, we’re neighbors, problem solvers, and dirt lovers at heart. Every project is a chance to roll up our sleeves, and get things done right. Because here, we believe that a little grit, a lot of care, and yes… sometimes rubbing a little dirt on it, goes a long way.
