Land Clearing for Builds, Cleanup, and Fire Safety

Temecula Valley Dirt Co. provides land clearing throughout Temecula Valley for homeowners, ranch properties, vacant lots, and construction sites. We clear brush, remove overgrowth, open up access, and prep land for grading, excavation, fencing, pads, and general site work.

Some customers call because they need defensible space and weed reduction. Others need a property cleaned up before a build, a sale, or a larger dirt project. Either way, we keep the scope practical: cut it back, haul it out, and leave the site ready for the next step.

Typical land clearing jobs often range from $2,000-$8,000+ depending on acreage, vegetation density, access, hauling needs, and whether the property also needs grading or dirt removal.
